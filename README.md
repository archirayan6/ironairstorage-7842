## About Iron-Air Batteries

Iron-air batteries are emerging as one of the most promising technologies for long-duration energy storage. They use iron, air, and water to store energy through reversible oxidation (rusting) processes, offering low-cost, scalable, and safer alternatives to lithium-ion systems for grid-scale renewable energy storage.
